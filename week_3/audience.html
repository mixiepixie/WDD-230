Target Audience

The audience for my chamber of commerce site will be small businesses in the bentonville area, as well as customers who are interested in exploring and supportiing new businesses, or just getting to know the area.

Persona 1

John is a business owner of a new icecream shop downtown. He is struggling to get customers. He wants to join a chamber of commerce to gain more traction in the community and to become a regular stop for tourists and members of the community. He would like to participate in events that the chamber of commerce hosts as a way of getting more involved in the community.

Persona 2

Hailey is a well-established business owner in the area. She would like to join a Chamber of Commerce to give back to her community and to have a voice in government affairs. She knows that by joining together with other business owners in the area through a chamber of commerce, she can make the difference she has been striving for for years!

Scenarios

  • How can I have a voice in my local government?
  • How can I gain more traction in my community as a stuggling business?
  • I am looking for small local businesses that pride themselves on customer service.
  • I am a tourist in the bentonville area. What are some hidden gems in this area?
  • Are there any community events I can participate in or volunteer at?
